# Piatek's WeaponMod

A content-packed Minecraft mod for **Minecraft 1.20.4** that adds new weapon types, special abilities, powerful legendary weapons, mining tools, custom crafting recipes, and more.

---

## Features

### New Weapon Classes

The mod adds four completely new weapon types, each available in all vanilla material tiers:

- Wooden
- Stone
- Iron
- Golden
- Diamond
- Netherite

### Weapon Types

## Sabre
Fast and agile weapon.

**Special Ability:**  
Every successful hit heals the player by **half a heart**.

---

## Warhammer
Heavy crushing weapon.

**Special Ability:**  
Launches enemies into the air and applies **Nausea**.

---

## Battleaxe
Extremely powerful melee weapon.

**Special Ability:**  
Deals massive damage with heavy attacks.

---

## Spear
Long-range melee weapon.

**Special Ability:**  
Deals more damage depending on attack range.  
*(Currently not fully working / experimental feature.)*

---

## Special Weapons

### Dragon Battleaxe
A legendary battleaxe infused with Ender Dragon power.

**Ability:**  
Shoots an Ender Dragon-style attack projectile.

---

### WitherWarhammer
A cursed warhammer powered by the Wither.

**Ability:**  
Shoots Wither skull projectiles.

---

### SonicSword
A weapon inspired by the Warden.

**Ability:**  
Releases a sonic attack similar to the Warden's sonic boom.

---

## Tools

### Master Pickaxe

An overpowered mining tool.

**Ability:**  
Mines blocks in a **3x3 area**.

---

## Extra Crafting Recipes

The mod also adds crafting recipes for:

- Mob Spawn Eggs
- Spawners
- Enchanted Golden Apple

---

## New Food

### Golden Steak
A special food item made for miners and adventurers.

---

## New Block

### Azbest
A new decorative/material block added by the mod.

---

## Crafting

All weapons, tools, and special items have their own crafting recipes in-game.

Use:
- Crafting Table
- Smithing upgrades (where applicable)

to obtain the new equipment.

- **Minecraft Version:** 1.20.4

## Installation

1. Install Minecraft **1.20.4**
2. Install the required mod loader
3. Place the mod `.jar` file into your `mods` folder
4. Launch the game
